Who We Are

EduGuide is a leading education consultancy dedicated to helping students find the right college and course for their career goals.

With over 10 years of experience in education counselling, we have successfully guided thousands of students in securing admissions to top colleges and universities across India.

We understand that choosing the right educational path is one of the most important decisions in a student's life. That's why our expert counsellors provide personalized guidance to help you make informed choices about your future.

Whether you're looking for engineering, medical, management, or distance education programs, we're here to support you every step of the way.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide honest, reliable, and personalized education counselling that empowers students to make the best decisions for their academic and professional future.

We aim to bridge the gap between students and quality education by offering expert guidance and complete admission support.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted education consultancy, helping every student discover their true potential and achieve their educational dreams.

We envision a future where every student has access to the right information and guidance to build a successful career.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is counselling really free?

Yes, our counselling services are completely free. We charge no fees for guidance and support. Our goal is to help students make informed decisions about their education.

How long does the counselling process take?

Initial counselling takes 30-45 minutes. The entire admission process depends on the college and course you choose. We stay with you throughout the journey.

Do you guarantee admission?

We provide expert guidance and maximize your chances, but admission depends on your eligibility and college selection criteria. We work honestly and transparently to help you succeed.

Which cities do you operate in?

We provide both online and offline counselling services. Our physical office is located in [Your City], but we serve students across India through phone and video consultations.

Can parents attend counselling sessions?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to participate in counselling sessions. Education decisions are important family decisions, and we value parent involvement.

What documents do I need for counselling?

For initial counselling, just bring your latest academic records (10th, 12th marksheets, or graduation documents). We'll guide you on additional documents needed for admission.
