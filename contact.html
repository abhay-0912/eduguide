Common Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We typically respond within 24 hours on working days. For urgent queries, please call us directly or WhatsApp us.

Is counselling available on weekends?

Yes! While our office is closed on Sundays, you can book Saturday appointments. We also offer online counselling sessions that can be scheduled according to your convenience.

Can I visit the office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure our counsellors can give you dedicated time without waiting.

Do you charge for counselling?

No! Our counselling services are completely FREE. There are no hidden charges.

Can I get counselling in regional languages?

Yes, our counsellors can provide guidance in Hindi, English, and other regional languages as applicable. Please mention your preferred language when contacting us.

How long does a counselling session take?

Initial counselling sessions typically take 30-45 minutes. Follow-up sessions may be shorter depending on your needs.

Can parents join the counselling session?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to participate in counselling sessions. Education decisions are important family matters.

Do you provide online counselling?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online counselling via video call, phone, or WhatsApp. Book your preferred mode of consultation.
